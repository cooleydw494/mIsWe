ABOUT M IS WE

M is We is a four piece post-punk band from Carrboro, NC. Starting as an experimental solo project of Michael Wood, it morphed into a full band, fusing elements of indie pop, new wave, dark wave, and punk. Lineup includes ex-members of Heavyweights, Something About Vampires and Sluts, and more.
